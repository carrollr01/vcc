New Tennis & Pickleball Program at VCC

We're excited to announce that beginning this summer, our VCC Tennis and Pickleball programs will be managed by the Baltimore Tennis Patrons (BTP), a non-profit community tennis association.

For 50 years, BTP has proudly served the Greater Baltimore area, offering award-winning programs to players of all ages and skill level - from recreational enthusiasts to competitive athletes. Now BTP is thrilled to bring that same energy and experience to our County Club!
