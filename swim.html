Swim Team

The goal of the Valley Gators Swim Team is for our swimmers to have fun and become stronger swimmers. The team is for club members eighteen and under who can swim the length of the pool. We are part of the Freestate Swim League. During the months of June and July we participate in swim meets against eight other local swim clubs.

Meet the Coaches

Brief intro on the coaches for the year

Andie Weitzman

Head Pro

We are excited to welcome Andie Weitzman as one of the coaches of the 2025 Valley Gators! Andie grew up swimming and swam at Towson University. She has been a lifeguard for six years and swim coach for five. She had coached children two - eighteen. Andie is currently a teacher for BCPS. She teaches fifth grade math and content. Andie's favorite stroke is breaststroke. When not a the pool she loves going to Orioles games with her roommates.

Mary Mazzulli

Junior Development

Mary grew up swimming for Valley and has proudly been coaching the team for the past five years. She is currently a student at the University of Pittsburgh, where she also competes on the club swim team. Her favorite strokes is backstroke. In addition to coaching at Valley, Mary is also one of the pool managers.

Owen Massie

Fitness & Agility

Owen Massie is returning for his 4th year, to be part of the coaching team for the 2025 Valley Gators. Owen began swimming with summer rec groups in Cincinnati, Ohio when he was 8 years old. He fell in love with the sport and decided to practice throughout the year on a competitive team. He moved to Baltimore in 2021, and joined the NBAC team. He swam with them for 3 years. He is now finishing his junior year at Dulaney HS. Owen is a certified as a Lifegaurd through the Red Cross. Owen loves playing games, working out, and is super excited to coach the Valley Gators again.

Ryan Blumenfeld

Doubles Specialist

Ryan grew up swimming for Valley and Loyola Blakefield Aquatics. He recently graduated from Colgate University where he was a member of their swim team and 5x record holder. Next year he will be attending Dartmouth College for an MPH and serve as a Graduate assistant for their program. His favorite stroke is butterfly. In addition to coaching at Valley, Ryan is working at the clubhouse, snack bar, and teaching swim lessons.

Volunteers

Family involvement is an integral part of summer swimming. We need volunteers in order to run our meets and parents are expected to volunteer for multiple meets. Volunteer job descriptions can be found here.

Announce each event and swimmer's name before each race
Help the swimmers 12 and under find their place before they race
Log swimmer's time into the computer at the scorer's table
Time each race using a stopwatch
Write the name and time of the winning swimmers
As each race finishes, record the sweep from the officials and the time from each lane
Record scores after each event and keep a running tally
