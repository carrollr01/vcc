A community staple since 1955.

Valley Country Club is conveniently located near the Ruxton, Riderwood, and Lutherville communities. With an Olympic-sized pool, large baby pool, and five tennis courts, Valley has provided generations of families with a fun atmosphere in which to spend their summers. From swim and tennis lessons, to swim team and inter-club tennis, there are activities at Valley for the whole family to enjoy.
